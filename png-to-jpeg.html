PNG to JPEG Conversion Tips

Learn how to get the best results when converting between these popular image formats

June 5, 2024

When to Use JPEG

JPEG is ideal for photographs and complex images with many colors. It uses lossy compression to significantly reduce file size while maintaining good visual quality.

May 22, 2024

Quality Comparison

While PNG is lossless, JPEG can achieve much smaller file sizes. For web use, JPEG quality of 70-80% often provides the best balance between quality and file size.

May 10, 2024

Background Tips

When converting transparent PNGs to JPEG, remember JPEG doesn't support transparency. Choose a solid background color that works well with your image.
