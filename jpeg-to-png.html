June 5, 2024

When to Use PNG

PNG is the best choice for images requiring transparency, such as logos, icons, and graphics with text. It uses lossless compression, meaning no quality is lost during saves.

May 22, 2024

Quality Comparison

While JPEG is great for photos, PNG maintains better quality for graphics with sharp edges and solid colors. PNG also supports alpha transparency for smooth edges.

May 10, 2024

Transparency Tips

When converting JPEG to PNG, use our transparency option to remove white backgrounds. This works best with logos and graphics that have clear contrast with their background.
