Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image converter

Is this image converter free to use?

Yes, our image converter is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges or premium features. You can convert as many images as you need without any limitations.

Are my images uploaded to your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (over 20MB) may cause performance issues in some browsers. For best results, we recommend converting images under 10MB.

Which browsers are supported?

Our converter works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. For AVIF conversion, Chrome and Edge work best.

Will the image quality be reduced after conversion?

For lossy formats like JPEG, there may be some quality reduction. PNG conversions are lossless. WEBP and AVIF conversions offer excellent quality at smaller file sizes.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Currently, our converter processes one image at a time. For batch conversions, you'll need to convert each image separately.

Why should I use WEBP or AVIF formats?

WEBP and AVIF are modern image formats that provide better compression than JPEG and PNG, resulting in smaller file sizes with similar or better quality.

Do you store my converted images?

No, we don't store any of your images. The conversion happens in your browser, and the result is immediately available for download without being stored anywhere.
